Our Story
With nearly three decades in the travel industry, our founder, Geetanjali, transformed her company from providing functional travel services to curating immersive, experiential journeys.

Follow Us

Enter: the global pandemic. Overnight, gaps in the healthcare workforce were exposed worldwide. Recognizing the urgent need for talent mobility across critical sectors and locations, Geetanjali, along with our co-founder, Amanda, envisioned a solution to address this demand-supply imbalance. The missing piece was upskilling and cultural adaptation—ensuring candidates could integrate and contribute effectively in new environments.
Life at Ed2Careers
We are an ambitious team of doers and builders, united by curiosity, tenacity, and a relentless drive for making meaningful change.

Our journey has already helped hundreds of individuals unlock their full potential, and we’re just getting started. As we continue to grow, we’re looking for equally passionate and driven individuals to join us.

Redefining Global Healthcare Mobility

At Ed2Careers, we’re proud to be the exclusive partner on the Global Workforce Mobility agenda with Artemis Hospitals. Together, we are reshaping healthcare training and bridging the gap between skilled Indian healthcare professionals and exciting opportunities in the UK healthcare sector.

About Artemis Hospitals

Artemis Hospitals, a JCI-accredited, multi-specialty healthcare facility in Gurugram (Gurgaon), Delhi, India, is a trusted name in delivering exceptional care. With 700 beds, state-of-the-art infrastructure, cutting-edge technology, and a dedicated team of medical professionals, Artemis stands as a beacon of quality healthcare for both national and international patients.

What Sets Apart our Program
State-of-the-Art Facilities
Artemis Hospitals offers world-class infrastructure and advanced medical technology, providing healthcare professionals with realistic, high-tech training environments to prepare for global healthcare challenges.
Credibility & Expertise
As a trusted and accredited partner, Artemis blends clinical excellence with international training standards. Our holistic approach also includes soft skills, language proficiency, and personality development through expert-led modules.
Proven Track Record

Ethical Recruitment Practices

Meet The Artemis Leadership Team

Dr. Devlina Chakravarty


Dr. Devlina Chakravarty, the patron of Artemis Hospitals, has been a transformative leader since 2007. Her vision emphasizes:

• Ethical healthcare practices.
• Transparency and trust as cornerstones of medical care.
• Personalized tertiary care with service excellence.
• Affordability, accessibility, and integrity as the foundation of sound healthcare delivery.

Her commitment to quality, efficiency, patient safety, and infection control has made Artemis Hospitals a leader in international healthcare training and mobility.

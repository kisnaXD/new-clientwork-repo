Ed2Careers: Shaping Globally-Ready Social Care Professionals

Indian nurses are respected worldwide for their clinical skills—but transitioning to international care roles require not just medical expertise but also a different personal approach and understanding of care environments . Our program helps nurses adapt to global care environments through focused training in communication, cultural sensitivity, and person-centred care along with the highest standards of clinical skills. In partnership with JCI accredited Artemis Hospitals and top social care certification providers, we offer pathways to rewarding careers in the UK, Ireland, Canada, and Australia.

What does the Training include?

Online Training

Live virtual coaching covering essential care and complex care topics such as dementia awareness, person-centered care, infection control, safety standards, social care compliance, mental capacity assessment etc.

In Person Training

Hands-on training by international coaches at Artemis Hospitals, New Delhi. This intensive training program focuses on the use of specialized medical equipment, advanced support techniques, and compassionate care for older individuals, including those living with dementia.

Cultural and Soft Skills Training

Indian healthcare professionals often face challenges adapting to the structured, patient- centric, and legally compliant systems overseas. This training equips them to confidently integrate into global healthcare environments and covers,

• Effective communication & amp; accent adaptation
• Cultural sensitivity & amp; legal awareness
• Clear clinical documentation
• Real-life care simulations
• Confidence, leadership & amp; teamwork under pressure

Language Proficiency

Tailored for healthcare professionals, our language training goes beyond generic courses—focusing on real-world communication, medical terminology, and workplace interactions. With emphasis on accents, nuances, and clinical vocabulary, candidates are well-prepared to integrate seamlessly into the healthcare system of the country

Our Selection Methodology and Vetting Process

Our rigorous multi-stage vetting process ensures every candidate meets the highest global standards, especially those defined by frameworks like the UK Skills for Care and the UK Government’s New-to-Care (Role Category A) principles.

Our selection process includes :

Comprehensive Background Checks

Full Document verification

Experience validation and skillset mapping

Empathy and Compassion

Reliability and Trustworthiness

Integrity
